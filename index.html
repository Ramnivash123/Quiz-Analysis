About Edu Learn

Edu Learn is an innovative quiz app designed to make learning engaging and effective. Our platform helps teachers create interactive quizzes while enabling students to attend them in a focused and enjoyable way.

With a mission to eliminate boredom and boost concentration, EduGreen transforms traditional quizzes into fun, interactive experiences. Our tools and features ensure that students stay engaged and teachers can easily track progress.
